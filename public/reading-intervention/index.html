Early Reading Intervention Support

ReadNest is being built for teachers and tutors who need a clear view of how young learners are practicing. Assigned teachers can review student activity history, missed items, progress snapshots, strengths, and needs practice.

Teacher Pro support

  • Assigned-student dashboard with learning signals.
  • Report card exports for parent conversations.
  • Intervention planning based on reading and activity history.
  • AI-ready workflow boundaries for future teacher insight support.

ReadNest is educational support, not a diagnostic service. Teachers should use it alongside professional judgment, classroom evidence, and local school requirements.
