First Grade Reading Practice

First grade readers are often building speed with sight words, decoding, and short sentence fluency. ReadNest gives them bite-sized practice that can repeat across the week while progress stays visible to caregivers and assigned teachers.

Practice areas

  • Sound blending and phonics patterns.
  • Sentence reading with read-aloud support.
  • Rhymes, sounds, and vocabulary activities.
  • Progress snapshots for strengths and needs practice.
