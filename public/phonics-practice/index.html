Phonics Practice for Early Readers

ReadNest supports phonics practice with simple sound chunks, read-aloud support, and word-to-sentence practice. Children can hear sounds, blend them together, and connect the word to a meaningful sentence.

Phonics skills supported

  • Short vowel sound practice.
  • Beginning and ending sound awareness.
  • Consonant blends and digraphs.
  • Chunking longer words into readable parts.
