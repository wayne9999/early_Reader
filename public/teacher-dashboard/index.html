Teacher Reading Dashboard

ReadNest Teacher Pro gives teachers a focused dashboard for assigned student reading progress. Teachers can review practice data, identify strengths and growth areas, and plan reading interventions based on sight word practice, fluency, memory games, consistency, and personalized path signals.

Teacher Pro tools

  • Classroom roster and student progress snapshots.
  • Strengths and weak areas by learner.
  • Reading intervention suggestions.
  • Backend AI-supported analysis with evidence-based recommendations and audit history when enabled.
