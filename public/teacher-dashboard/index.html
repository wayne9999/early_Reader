Teacher Reading Dashboard

ReadNest is being structured with a teacher dashboard for classroom reading progress. Teachers can review student practice data, identify strengths and growth areas, and plan reading interventions based on sight word practice, fluency, memory games, and consistency.

Planned teacher tools

  • Classroom roster and student progress snapshots.
  • Strengths and weak areas by learner.
  • Reading intervention suggestions.
  • Backend AI analysis with evidence-based recommendations and audit history.
