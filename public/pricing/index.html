ReadNest Pricing

ReadNest starts with useful free practice, then offers paid plans for families and teachers who need deeper personalization, clearer progress insight, and more guided support.

Free Reader

Includes Reading, Memory, Rhymes, Sounds, Sentences, and basic personalized dashboard signals for signed-in student accounts.

Family Plus

Family Plus is $7 per month and unlocks the deeper personalized student path, including Story Steps, Word Garden, printable plans, cloud sync support, and future premium packs.

Teacher Pro

Teacher Pro is $19 per month and unlocks classroom dashboard access, assigned-student analysis, report exports, intervention planning, and AI-supported recommendations when enabled.
