Reading Progress Tracking for Parents and Caregivers

ReadNest helps caregivers see how a child is practicing reading over time and what to try next. Progress includes known sight words, sentence reading sessions, memory board completions, recent misses, strengths, and daily practice goals.

Progress insights

  • Known words and repeated practice.
  • Reading session completion.
  • Memory game completion and improvement.
  • Recommended next steps for short home practice.
  • Personalized path suggestions based on grade, goal, and history.
