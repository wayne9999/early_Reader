Personalized Reading Practice for Kids

ReadNest gives early elementary learners short reading practice sessions that can adapt around grade level, reading goals, recent misses, sight words, phonics blending, sentence reading, and fluency. The app is designed for kindergarten, first grade, and second grade children who need calm, repeatable literacy practice.

What children practice

  • Recognizing high-frequency sight words.
  • Blending sounds into words.
  • Reading short age-appropriate sentences.
  • Listening to words and sentences with read-aloud support.
  • Following a next-best practice path based on recent progress.

Who it helps

ReadNest can support parents, caregivers, tutors, and teachers who want playful reading practice plus clearer progress signals at home, in tutoring, or in an elementary classroom.
