Online Reading Games for Kids

ReadNest gives young learners short, playful reading games that fit into a 5 to 10 minute routine. Kids can practice sight words, phonics sounds, rhyming, sentence building, and memory skills without needing a long lesson.

Free starter activities

  • Reading practice with large words and read-aloud support.
  • Memory matching games for attention and working memory.
  • Signed-in practice for Rhymes, Sounds, and Sentences.

Built for repeat practice

The activities are intentionally short so families, tutors, and teachers can use ReadNest during homework time, tutoring sessions, classroom rotations, or a quick daily reading habit.
