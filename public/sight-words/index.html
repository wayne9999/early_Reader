How sight word practice works

Children see a word, hear it, blend or chunk it, and read it in a sentence.

  • Large readable word cards.
  • Listen controls for words and sentences.
  • Short sentences that match the current word.
  • Progress tracking for known and repeated words.

Built for daily review

Sight word practice works best when it is frequent and brief. ReadNest is designed for a few minutes at a time.

What are sight words?

Sight words are common words children learn to recognize quickly so reading becomes smoother.

Does ReadNest show words in sentences?

Yes. Reading practice includes short sentence examples tied to the current word.
